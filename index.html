HOW TO PLAYA = BACK
STATS
Roll 5 dice: ATK · DEF · GDP × 3 (summed). Shake, then release.
FAVORED FACTION
One faction die is rolled before the game. Any player rolling that faction gains +3 ATK that turn.
PHASE 1 — FACTION
Roll all 3 faction dice. Need 2 matching symbols. A = shake all (3 rolls max). B = declare your faction.
PHASE 2 — EQUIP
Tap equip zone to roll both dice. Each ⊕ = 1 activation. Tap your faction die to spend each equip as ATK or DEF. 2 end-turns = turn over. 3 rolls max.
DAMAGE
Output = (ATK acts × ATK stat) + flat bonuses. Absorbed = DEF acts × DEF stat. GDP lost = output − absorbed.
WIN
Reduce opponent to 0 GDP 3 times. 3 falls = eliminated.
